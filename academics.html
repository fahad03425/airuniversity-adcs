ADCS Program

The Department of Computer Science offers a 2-year Associate Degree Program in Computer Science and is equivalent to a 14-year Bachelor Degree.

Candidates holding Associate Degree can continue towards respective BS 4-year degree program. Or the student can apply for new admission towards any BS 4-Year degree program (Subject to admission eligibility with exemption/transfer of relevant qualified courses) as per policy.

Program Objectives

1 Enlighten students with fundamentals of theoretical Computer Science.

2 Enhance the programming skills of students by exposing them to programming languages, tools, technologies.

3 Enhance the analytical and problem-solving skills of students.

4 Enable students with technical expertise to add fruitful contribution in the software industry.

5 Provide students with a combination of knowledge, hands-on experience, and application of theory to information.

ADCS Program Description

Provide quality education and produce highly skilled professionals in areas of Computer Science and Information Technology.
